The Math

  • 1 trillion trees divided by 8 billion people on Earth = 125 trees per person
  • Doing it within a decade means 12.5 trees per year
  • That's roughly 1 tree per month!

By planting one tree every month, you’re directly contributing to a livable planet for future generations.
